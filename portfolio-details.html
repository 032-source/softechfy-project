Project Solutions

To accomplish the client's goal, we executed a comprehensive UI/UX design and web development strategy that included the following key steps. Each phase was carefully planned and implemented to ensure high-performance and alignment with our client's vision.

1

UI/UX Design

We designed a clean and navigable UI/UX that made the platform easy to use for all users and mental healthcare experts. Everything, from colors to fonts, was picked to make the website feel calm, easy to use, and professional.

2

Web Development

We created a fast and responsive website with a backend to support smooth performance. The Geo-targeted search feature was integrated to help users with accurate and location-driven results quickly.

Backend:
3

API Integration

We added helpful tools (APIs) that let different parts of the system talk to each other and share information instantly. This ensured faster results, accurate listings, and a great user experience.

4

Listing Functionality

We developed a smart listing feature to manage mental health professionals clearly. Users could easily browse, filter, and find experts based on location, availability, and specialization.

Registration

Mental health professionals could join by sharing their basic info and qualifications.

Login

A secure login system was put in place for professionals to manage their profiles easily.

Listing in directory

Once approved by the platform, they were listed in the directory for users to find them based on location and specialization.

5

Location & Profession Based Search Functionality

We made a feature that helps users find mental health professionals near their location. This feature included smart filters to connect with nearby experts based on their unique needs.

6

Support & Maintenance

We kept updating the platform to ensure it stayed safe, up-to-date, and problem-free. Regular audits and performance improvements were done to deliver the best result.
